Feedback is a crucial process that provides an opportunity to give opinions or insights about a task, project, or action. It not only helps in improving performance but also offers chances for growth and learning. Positive feedback encourages motivation, while constructive feedback helps address weaknesses and enhance skills. Feedback is a two-way process where it is essential to give and receive opinions with honesty and openness, ensuring continuous development and improvement.
